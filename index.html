Frequently Asked Questions

Is my guard housekeeping manpower of “quality”?
Quality depends on the recruitment process, training, and management. Ensure your provider follows strict vetting and training procedures.
Payment! Payment! Why are these manpower outsourcing companies perpetually short of payments?
Payment issues often arise due to delayed client payments or mismanagement. Always review contracts and payment terms before hiring.
Why does security agency make the “poor” guard do double duty?
Some agencies exploit guards due to staff shortages. Always ensure your agency follows labor laws.
Is the salary break up of your employees, labour-law compliant?
Salaries must comply with government labor laws, including minimum wage, overtime, and benefits.
How to select a security services company that is right for you?
Check licenses, reviews, experience, and compliance with labor laws.
